SSL
The connection to the SilenceIM website should use a valid, trusted server certificate.
PGP keys
Generate new PGP keypair every new session. Re-generate PGP keypair every 30 minutes of chatting.
Keep your private key as secret as possible. Share your public key with your buddies.
AES/RC4
Do not send AES/RC4 passwords to your buddies via public channel or network.
Monitor traffic
Use WebSockets traffic inspector in browser or any packet analyzer (like Wireshark) to ensure that your messages are encrypted.
Use TOR
Hide your identity over the TOR network.
